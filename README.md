# SkillMate


What it is: A social learning and skill-sharing platform that connects people who want to learn, teach, or collaborate based on shared skills and interests.

Core purpose: To help users discover skill partners, teachers, learners, or hobby buddies â€” turning individual skills into meaningful connections and community growth.

Teach, Learn & Partner: Users can offer their skills, find someone to learn from, or team up with others for activities/hobbies.

Profile system: Create a profile that showcases your skills, portfolio, and interests to attract the right matches.

In-app messaging: Communicate directly on the platform without sharing personal contact info.

Rating & trust: Users can rate each other after interactions, helping build credibility and a trusted community.

Filters & discovery: Find the right connections using filters like distance, age, and skill type.

Community focus: Encourages both personal growth and social engagement through collaboration and skill exchange.
